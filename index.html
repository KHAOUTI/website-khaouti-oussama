JUJUTSU KAISEN

"Jujutsu Kaisen (JJK) is a gripping manga series by Gege Akutami, following the adventures of Yuji Itadori, a high schooler with remarkable strength. When he swallows a cursed object, Yuji enters a world of sorcery, battling malevolent spirits alongside other sorcerers. With its dynamic action and intriguing characters, Jujutsu Kaisen delivers a thrilling supernatural tale packed with suspense and excitement."

OSAMA RANKING

"Osama Ranking, created by Sosuke Toka, presents a unique narrative centered around Bojji, a deaf and powerless prince who is determined to prove his worth and claim his rightful place as the king. Through heartfelt storytelling and vibrant artwork, this manga explores themes of identity, resilience, and the power of perseverance in the face of adversity."

CLASSROOM OF THE ELITE

"Classroom of the Elite, written by Syougo Kinugasa and illustrated by Shunsaku Tomose, is a thought-provoking light novel and manga series. Set in an elite high school where students are groomed to become future leaders, the story follows Kiyotaka Ayanokoji, a seemingly average student with hidden talents. As Kiyotaka navigates the complexities of the school's strict hierarchy and uncovers dark secrets, he forms alliances and confronts challenges that test his intelligence and resolve. With its intriguing premise and intricate plot twists, Classroom of the Elite offers a compelling exploration of power, manipulation, and the pursuit of excellence in education."
